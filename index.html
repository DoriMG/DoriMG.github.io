Biography

I am a PhD student in the 4-year PhD programme at Sussex University. I am doing my project in the Brain Energy Lab with Catherine Hall. I study place cells using two-photon microscopy and look how they respond to changes in a virtual reality environment.

Outside the lab I like to write about science and diversity and inclusion in STEM. I write for Massive Science and other outlets.
